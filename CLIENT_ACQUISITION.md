## 🎯 WHERE TO FIND THEM

### LinkedIn (Best Channel)
Search filters:
- Title: "Real Estate Agent", "Realtor", "Real Estate Broker"
- Location: Any US city (start with Dallas, Houston, Miami, Phoenix — high agent density)
- Company size: 1-10 (solo/small team — decision makers)

Look for: Agents who post actively, mention missing calls, or talk about being busy

### Instagram/Facebook
- Search hashtags: #realtor #realtorlife #realestate
- Find agents posting "just got back from a showing" — they're literally the problem
- Facebook Groups: "Real Estate Agents Network", "Realtors Facebook Group"

### Cold Email
- Use Hunter.io or Apollo.io (free tiers) to find emails
- Search: "{name} realtor {city} email"
- Many agents list email on Zillow, Realtor.com, or their personal site

## 🎤 DEMO CALL SCRIPT (15 min)

**Min 0-2:** Build rapport
> "Thanks for making time. Quick question before I show you anything — when you're with a client and your phone rings, what do you usually do?"

**Min 2-5:** Deepen the pain
> "And how often does that happen in a week? ... Have you ever lost a deal because of that?"

**Min 5-10:** Show the product
> "Let me show you what VoiceDesk does. I'm going to play you a real call — this is the AI handling an incoming lead inquiry..."
*(Play a demo recording from voicedesk.online)*

**Min 10-13:** The ask
> "For $97/month, it's handling every call you miss. That's less than you probably spend on Zillow leads that don't pick up the phone. Does this feel relevant to your situation?"

**Min 13-15:** Close or next step
> "Want to get it set up? Takes about 20 minutes to configure. Or if you want to think about it, I can send you a trial link."

## 💡 OBJECTION HANDLING

**"I already have an answering service"**
> "Those are humans reading scripts — $20+/hr, limited hours. VoiceDesk is AI, always on, and costs $97/month flat. How much are you paying now?"

**"My clients want to talk to me personally"**
> "Totally understand. The AI doesn't replace you — it catches calls when you physically can't answer and books them for a callback with you. You still close the relationship."

**"I'm not sure it'll sound natural"**
> "Fair — let me just play you 60 seconds of it on a real call. You can judge for yourself."

**"Too expensive"**
> "One extra closing from a lead you would've missed pays for 2-3 years of this. What's your average commission?"
